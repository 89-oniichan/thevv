# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 20, 2024**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our Discord bot (the "Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information
- Discord User ID (for identifying users)
- Discord Username (for display purposes)
- Discord Server (Guild) ID (to provide server-specific services)
- Command usage data (to operate the Bot)

### 2.2 Information You Provide
- Messages sent to the Bot through commands
- Order numbers and related information when tracking orders
- Any information voluntarily provided through Bot interactions

### 2.3 WooCommerce Store Data
- Product information (names, prices, descriptions, images)
- Order information (when you request order tracking)
- Customer information associated with orders you query

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide Bot functionality and respond to your commands
- Connect your Discord account with your WooCommerce orders
- Display product information and process inquiries
- Improve the Bot's performance and features
- Troubleshoot issues and provide support

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is processed in real-time to respond to commands
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect data
- User data is not permanently stored unless necessary for Bot functionality
- WooCommerce data is accessed via secure API connections

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only:
- With Discord (as required for the Bot to function)
- With your WooCommerce store (to retrieve order and product information)
- When required by law or legal process

## 6. Data Retention

- Command data is typically processed and discarded immediately
- Temporary cache data may be stored for performance optimization
- No long-term storage of personal messages or user data

## 7. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with:
- **Discord**: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
- **WooCommerce**: Subject to your store's privacy policy and WooCommerce terms

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request information about data the Bot has processed
- Request deletion of any stored data related to you
- Stop using the Bot at any time
- Block the Bot from accessing your information

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age (or older if required by local law). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

This Bot complies with:
- Discord's Developer Terms of Service
- Discord's Developer Policy
